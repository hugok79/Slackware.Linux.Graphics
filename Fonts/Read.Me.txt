OTF fonts in this directory listed as follows are from the GNU FreeFont project release on 3th of May 2012. 

* Free*.otf 
* Free*.Bold.otf 
* Free*.Oblique.otf 
* Free*.Bold.Oblique.otf 

You can use these fonts in most modern operating systems such as GNU/Linux, UNIX, BSD, OS X and Windows. 

In GNU/Linux Slackware: 

* For system wide use of the above OTF fonts, copy them into "/usr/share/fonts/OTF/" directory. 
* For user specific use of the fonts, copy them into "~/.fonts/" directory.
